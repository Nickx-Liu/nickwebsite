Overview

18th - 19th Century

After America won the Revolutionary War in 1783, the belief of Manifest Destiny had the US involved in the Mexican-American War and the Spanish-American War to expand their territory. The United States would also endure the War of 1812 and the Civil War during this period.

20th Century

The 20th Century had seen the bloodiest and most costly wars in the history of the world. During the 20th Century, the United States was involved in both world wars, the Korean and Vietnam War, and the Cold War; this made the 20th Century the deadliest time in American History, with over 1.5 million American casualties.

21st Century

American conflicts in the 21st Century greatly revolved around America's War on Terror in response to the 9/11 attacks and civil wars in the Middle East to counter terrorism and instability in the region. These conflicts include the War in Afghanistan, the Iraq War, and the War on ISIL.
